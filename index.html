Our Story

Superteam Malaysia is the local chapter of the global Superteam network — the most elite community of talent in the Solana ecosystem. We launched because Malaysia has extraordinary builders who deserve a real home.

"We build the infrastructure for Malaysia's Web3 generation."

From the shadow of the Petronas Towers to the hawker stalls of Penang, our builders are shipping products felt across the globe. If you're building on Solana in Malaysia — you belong here.

FAQ

Got
Questions?

Everything you need to know about Superteam Malaysia.

Still curious? Join us →
What is Superteam Malaysia?
+
Superteam Malaysia is the local chapter of the global Superteam network — the most elite community of talent in the Solana ecosystem. We connect Malaysian builders, creators, and founders with real opportunities to earn, learn, and ship on Solana.
How do I join?
+
Apply through our application form — reviewed on a rolling basis. We look for people passionate about Web3 who are actively building. Join our Telegram group to start connecting right away.
What opportunities are available?
+
We offer bounties, hackathons with cash prizes, grants for early-stage projects, job referrals, mentorship, and ecosystem connections. Opportunities range from a few hundred to tens of thousands of ringgit.
How can projects collaborate with us?
+
Solana projects and ecosystem partners can post bounties, sponsor events, or partner with us to access Malaysian talent. Reach out via Twitter/X or Telegram.
Do I need to be a developer?
+
Not at all. Superteam welcomes developers, designers, content creators, growth hackers, BD professionals, and community managers. If you're passionate about building Web3 in Malaysia, you belong here.
Is it free to join?
+
Membership is free. We're selective to maintain quality, but there are no fees. Once you're in, all resources, events, and community access are yours.
